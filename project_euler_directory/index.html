Problem 6: The sum of the squares of the first ten natural numbers is, 1^2 + 2^2 + … + 10^2 = 385 The square of the sum of the first ten natural numbers is, (1 + 2 + … + 10)^2 = 552 = 3025 Hence the difference between the sum of the squares of the first ten natural numbers and the square of the sum is 3025 − 385 = 2640. Find the difference between the sum of the squares of the first one hundred natural numbers and the square of the sum.Solution
